# System Overview

The **Smart Traffic Management System (SMTS)** is an advanced urban infrastructure management platform designed to monitor, simulate, and optimize traffic flow in real-time. By combining IoT sensor data, agent-based simulation, and Local Large Language Models (LLMs), SMTS provides a holistic view of city mobility and actionable insights for traffic controllers.

## 🌟 Core Mission

To reduce urban congestion, improve emergency response times, and enhance the overall efficiency of city transport networks through intelligent automation and human-centric data visualization.

## 🛠 Key Capabilities

### 1. Real-time Monitoring & Visualization
*   **Dynamic Map**: A high-fidelity interactive map displaying road segments, intersections, and traffic density.
*   **Live Metrics**: Real-time tracking of vehicle counts, average speeds, and congestion levels.
*   **Multi-Layer Views**: Toggle between urban density, institutional zones, and traffic flow layers.

### 2. AI-Driven Optimization (Ollama Integration)
*   **Smart Signal Control**: Automated adjustment of traffic signal phases based on live congestion data.
*   **Congestion Prediction**: Predictive analysis of traffic bottlenecks 15-60 minutes into the future.
*   **Insight Generation**: Natural language summaries of traffic status and recommended interventions.

### 3. Gamified Traffic Simulation
*   **Interactive Sandbox**: A dedicated `/simulation` environment where users can manually manage traffic signals and respond to events.
*   **Dynamic Spawning**: Realistic vehicle generation based on zone profiles (Residential, Commercial, Industrial).
*   **Optimization Scoring**: Real-time XP and city-wide optimization metrics to track system performance.

### 4. Incident Management
*   **Rapid Reporting**: Tools for reporting accidents, road closures, and flood risks.
*   **Impact Analysis**: Visualizing how an incident on one segment ripples through the surrounding network.
*   **Audit Logging**: Comprehensive history of all system overrides and incident resolutions.

## 👥 User Roles

*   **Traffic Controller**: Full access to signal overrides, incident management, and predictive analytics.
*   **Driver/Public**: Access to real-time traffic status and route optimization suggestions (limited views).
